WE ARE SERVING CUSTOMERS THROUGHOUT THE WORLD, EXPORTING WIDE VARIETY OF VEHICLES
S&H Motors was established in 1991 as a Japanese Automobile Trading Company under the Used Car Dealer Registration Number 452750009801. S&H Motors is experienced in commercial and non-commercial Japanese vehicles exporting and has been renowned as a one-stop-shop in vehicle exporting industry. S&H Motors are proud members of Yamato Chamber of Commerce, Kanagawa public Safety Commission.

Follow 4 easy steps to own your vehicle.

How To Buy used vehicles from Japan

 

Search Cars & Trucks

Choose your favourite vehicle from our Stock List. After select your vehicle you can send us your Inquiry by click on Send Inquiry button.

 

Make Payment

After getting the proforma invoice and confirmation of vehicle, please make the payment to our bank account.

 

Shipment & Documents

We will arrange shipping and will send shipping schedule and other details via fax or email. The original documents will be sent to you by express courier

 

Receive Vehicle

Please contact your local customs agent for customs clearance and enjoy the car from your port.
