Welcome to
S&H Motors
We provide services to customers around the globe, exporting a diverse range of vehicles.
We are being in the field of Japanese Vehicle and their parts from the beginning of 1990s. And At the time of March 2014 we established a new company under the name of S&H (Motors) Trading company limited to meet and reach our wide range of clients all over the world. S&H Motors have experienced in all type of vehicles such as commercial, non-commercial, luxury cars and all kind of any vehicles parts. S&H Motors are proud member of TAA Auction (Toyota Auto Auction), CAA Auction, USS Auction, JU Auction, KCAA Auction, Honda Auction, Mirive Auction, IMA, Bay Auc Auction and AUCNET Auto Auction.
S&H Motors is one of the best and customers trusted vehicle exporting company and have gained a very good reputation in the South and East African, Caribbean, Middle East, European, Oceanian, North American markets. We are providing our service throughout the world, exporting wide variety of vehicles & their parts.
Our experience staff is committed towards continuous improvement of service & quality. As a result of this, we think we will expand more our service through all over the world in coming years. Since our business in mostly consist of online auction and we are more committed to fulfill the requirement of our clients at high priority basis.
